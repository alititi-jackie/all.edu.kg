About All EDU

All EDU is an open and independent education initiative dedicated to providing free, high-quality educational resources and tools for students, teachers, developers, and lifelong learners around the world.

Based in Kyrgyzstan, our mission is to empower knowledge-sharing, collaboration, and educational accessibility through technology.

Free Tools & Resources

  • Student access to JetBrains IDEs
  • Canva for Education
  • Autodesk software for learning
  • GitHub Student Developer Pack (soon)
  • Free online learning materials (OpenCourseWare, MOOCs, etc.)
